My Superpower

Power Mimicry

What is Power Mimicry?

Power mimicry is the ability to replicate powers belonging to other people through physical touch. Power mimicry can be used several ways. From completely replicating a person's powers to use as one's own or even using a person's powers' against them.

How I Got This Ability

I was born with this ability. And as someone who doesn't know anyone else with this ability it is very hard to control my power. Through trial and error I have begun to understand that my power does not work unless I truly have a reason or I know what I want to do with it.

What I Do With My Ability

Having an ability such as power mimicry can be extremely useful. While it of course has its drawbacks, I find that the advantages of this power outweigh them. I can use them to make sure I don't get injured even though I'm extremely clumsy. Or I can use them to help me study in a short amount of time.
